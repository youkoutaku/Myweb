I'm trying to make a blog

2023/3/11

Why

Last week, I wanted to submit a ES to a company. It's time to start the job search I thought, although it's likely I'll be moving on to a master's program. But the company asked me to provide a website that shows persenal sikiles, achievements, etc. I just typed my GitHub's URL on it. So this time, I really want to have a blog to help others understand me. And I also love sharing some of my learning notes. They're why I got start to make this website.

How

At first, after reading some suggestions online, I chose the book "Head First HTML And CSS". It took me about 3~4 hours a day for a week, reading, writing code and taking notes at the same time. After that I found some very nice blogs which gave me a lot of inspiration on the design and layout of the look and feel. I spent a day, mostly on making the navigation bar, even though it was rudimentary.
